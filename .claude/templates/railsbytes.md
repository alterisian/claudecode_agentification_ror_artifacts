# Rails Bytes

**Site:** https://railsbytes.com/
**Command:** `rails app:template LOCATION=https://railsbytes.com/script/...`

## Best for
Existing projects, or new projects where you want to compose features à la carte.

## What you get
- A library of small, focused templates (auth, error tracking, CI config, etc.)
- Works on new and existing Rails apps
- Community-contributed, broad coverage

## Trade-offs
- Not a single cohesive starting point — you assemble pieces yourself
- Template quality varies; review before running
- No built-in billing or team accounts
